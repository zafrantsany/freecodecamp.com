Ir Soekarno

The founding father of Indonesia

Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1901- Born in Surabaya, Indonesia.
  • 1920- Graduated from Hoogere Burger School.
  • 1926- Graduated from THS (Technische Hoogeschool) in Bandung City. Then with Ir. Anwari, Soekarno founded the Engineering Bureau.
  • 1927- Soekarno founded PNI (Partai Nasional Indonesia)". PNI is a party that aims to free the Indonesia Nation.
  • 1929- Soekarno was imprisoned in the Sukamiskin prison by the Dutch colonial.
  • 1945- Together with Mohammad Hatta and several other figures, he declared Indonesia nation's independence
  • 1944- Rejects a 100% salary increase from Dupont, leaves behind his pregnant wife, and flies to Mexico to head a new plant pathology program. Over the next 16 years, his team breeds 6,000 different strains of disease resistent wheat - including different varieties for each major climate on Earth.
  • 1945- Discovers a way to grown wheat twice each season, doubling wheat yields
  • 1970- Soekarno died at the Army Hospital on June 21, 1970 due to illness that continued to worsen. He was buried in Blitar, close to the grave of his mother, Ida Ayu Nyoman Rai
